## Challenge
Sketch the set `$S$` of points in the complex plane satisfying the given inequality. Determine whether the set is open, closed, a domain, bounded or connected.

   a) `${\rm Re}(z) < -1$`
   b) `$-1 \leq {\rm Im}(z) < 4$`
   c) `$|z-i| > 1$`
