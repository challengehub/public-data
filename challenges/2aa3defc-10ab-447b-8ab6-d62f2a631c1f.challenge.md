## Challenge
Determine the angle between the two vectors `$\bm{a} = [3,0,0]$` and `$\bm{b} = [3,1,0]$` and use it to calculate `$\bm{c} = \bm{a} \times \bm{b}$`. Which direction does the vector `$\bm{c}$` point?
