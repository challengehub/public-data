## Challenge
Derive an expression for the Fourier coefficient `$a_k$` in
`$$ f(t) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{k=1}^{N} a_k \cos(2 \pi k t) + b_k \sin(2 \pi k t) $$`

Note the difference in period between the above equation and the suggested resource.
