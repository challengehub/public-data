## Challenge
Considering a vector `$\bm{a} = \hat{k}$` and a vector `$\bm{b} = \hat{i}$`, show that `$\bm{a} \bm{\times} (\bm{a} \bm{\times} \bm{b}) = -a^2 \bm{b}$`.
