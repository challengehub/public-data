## Challenge
Determine `$u_t$` and `$u_{xx}$` for the equation

`$$ u(x,t) = 5tx^2 + 3t - x $$`

## Solution Form
To check your answer, substitute `$x=3$` and `$t=2$` into your answers, as appropriate, and enter the sum the two answers.
