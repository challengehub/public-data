## Challenge
Determine `$u_t$` and `$u_{yy}$` for the equation

`$$ u(y,t) = 5ty^2 + 3t - y $$`

## Solution Form
To check your answer, substitute `$y=3$` and `$t=2$` into your answers, as appropriate, and enter the sum the two answers.
