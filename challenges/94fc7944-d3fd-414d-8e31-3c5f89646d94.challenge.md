## Challenge
Considering the predator-prey system of equations below with the given coefficient values, calculate the total population of the system at equilibrium.

- `$\alpha=\gamma=100$`
- `$\beta=\delta=0.1$`

`$$
\begin{aligned}
  x' &= \alpha x - \beta x y \\
  y' &= \delta xy - \gamma y
\end{aligned}
 $$`
