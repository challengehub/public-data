## Challenge
Show that the following limits do *not* exist.

a) `$\displaystyle{\lim_{z \to 0}}\ \frac{z}{|z|}$` 
    
b) `$\displaystyle{\lim_{z \to 0}}\ \frac{\mathrm{Re}(z) \mathrm{Im}(z)}{{|z|}^2}$` 
    
c) `$\displaystyle{\lim_{z \to 0}}\ {({z}/{\bar{z}})}^2$`
