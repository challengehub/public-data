## Challenge
Explain why the following sets are **connected** or **not connected**.

**1 point**:
`$$ 
A = \{z \in \Complex ~|~ 0 \lt Re(z) \lt 4 \}
$$`

**2 points**:
`$$ 
B = \{z \in \Complex ~|~ |z| \lt \} \cap \{|z -2i| < 1\} 
$$`

**4 points**:
`$$ 
C = \{z \in \Complex |~~|z| < 1\} \backslash \{0\}
$$`

**8 points**:
`$$ 
D = \{z \in \Complex\}
$$`
