## Challenge
Find the solution for the system of equations
`$$
  \bm{x'} = \left(
    \begin{array}{cc}
      -4 & 0 \\
       0 & -4
    \end{array}
  \right) \bm{x}
$$`

given the condition

`$$
\bm{x}(3) = \left(
    \begin{array}{c}
       5 \\
      -2 
    \end{array}
  \right)
$$`


## Solution Form

To check your solution, sum the numbers in the vector `$x(0.9)$`.
