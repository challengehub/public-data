## Challenge
The complex Fourier series of a function is given by 

`$$
    f(t) = \sum_{k=-\infty}^{\infty} c_k e^{i 2 \pi k t}
 $$`

Derive an expression for the complex Fourier coefficients, `$c_k$`.
