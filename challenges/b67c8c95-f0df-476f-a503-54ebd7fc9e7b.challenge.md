## Challenge
Obtain expressions for the tangent curves of each curve below and calculate the angle between them.

`$\gamma_1 = e^{it}$` on the contour `$0<t<\pi$`
`$\gamma_2 = t$` on the contour `$\frac12<t<\pi$`
