## Challenge
Using your knowledge of the Fourier transform for window and triangle functions, calculate the Fourier Transform for the rocket function:

`$$
    f(t)=
    \begin{cases}
        2 - |t| & \text{for } 0 \le |t| < \frac{1}{2}\\
        1 - |t| & \text{for } \frac{1}{2} \le |t| \le 1\\
        0 & \text{otherwise}
    \end{cases}
$$`
