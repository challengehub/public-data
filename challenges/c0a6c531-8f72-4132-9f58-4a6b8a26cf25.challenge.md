## Challenge

The following 6 equations correspond to the 6 graphs below. Place the graphs in the order of the equations shown here.

1. `$y'=x/10$`
2. `$y'=0.1y$`
3. `$y'=0.2y(1+y/6)$`
4. `$y'=(x-y)/(x+y)$`
5. `$y'=2(y-1)/x$`
6. `$y'=2y/(x+5)$`
