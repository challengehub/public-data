## Challenge
A triangle function of general width can be defined as
`$$
    f(at)=
    \begin{cases}
        1 - a|t| & \text{for } a|t| < 1\\
        0 & \text{otherwise}
    \end{cases}
$$`

Calculate the Fourier Transform `$\hat{f}(s)$` for the case when `$a=1/2$`.
