## Challenge

Sum the points of the differential equations that have characteristic equations with complex roots.

**1 point**: `$−3y'' − 5y' + 2y = 0$`

**2 points**: `$3y'' − 4y' + 3y = 0$`

**4 points**: `$3y'' − 6y' + 3y = 0$`

**8 points**: `$3y'' − 5y' + 2y = 0$`

**16 points**: `$3y'' − 5y' + 4y = 0$`

**32 points**: `$3y'' + 5y' + 2y = 0$`
