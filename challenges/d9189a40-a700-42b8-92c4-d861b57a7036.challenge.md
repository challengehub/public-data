## Comments
Our first step is to make sure that there is no confusion when calculating with complex numbers.


## Challenge
What can be said about the components `$x$` and `$y$` of the complex number `$z=x+iy$` if 

1. `$z = \overline z$`?
2. `$(z)^2 = (\overline z)^2$` ?
