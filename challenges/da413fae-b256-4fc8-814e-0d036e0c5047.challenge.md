## Challenge
Consider the function

`$$f(z)=\frac{2z}{(z-i)(z+3)}$$`

Sum the points for the cases below that can be expanded in terms of a **Taylor series**:

**1 point:** `$|z|<1$`

**2 points:** `$|z|>1$`

**4 points:** `$1<|z|<2$`

**8 points:** `$1<|z|<3$`

**16 points:** `$1<|z|<4$`

**32 points:** `$2<|z|<3$`

**64 points:** `$|z-5|<2$`

**128 points:** `$|z+i|<2$`

## Resources
