## Challenge
Add the points of the following ODE systems that are *non-homogeneous*:

1 point: `$
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1' \\
        x_2' \\
        x_3'
    \end{array}
\right)
=
\left(
    \begin{array}{ccc}
        1 & 2 & 3 \\
        4 & 5 & 6 \\
        7 & 8 & 9
    \end{array}
\right)
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1 \\
        x_2 \\
        x_3
    \end{array}
\right)
+
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        0 \\
        0 \\
        0
    \end{array}
\right)
$`

2 points: `$
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1' \\
        x_2' \\
        x_3'
    \end{array}
\right)
=
\left(
    \begin{array}{ccc}
        1 & 2 & 3 \\
        4 & 5 & 6 \\
        7 & 8 & 9
    \end{array}
\right)
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1 \\
        x_2 \\
        x_3
    \end{array}
\right)
+
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        1 \\
        0 \\
        0
    \end{array}
\right)$`

4 points:`$
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1' \\
        x_2' \\
        x_3'
    \end{array}
\right)
=
\left(
    \begin{array}{ccc}
        1 & 2 & 3 \\
        4 & 5 & 6 \\
        7 & 8 & 9
    \end{array}
\right)
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1 \\
        x_2 \\
        x_3
    \end{array}
\right)
+
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        1 \\
        \sin(t) \\
        0
    \end{array}
\right)$`

8 points:`$
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1' \\
        x_2' \\
        x_3'
    \end{array}
\right)
=
\left(
    \begin{array}{ccc}
        1 & 2 & 3 \\
        4 & 5 & 6 \\
        7 & 8 & 9
    \end{array}
\right)
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        x_1 \\
        x_2 \\
        x_3
    \end{array}
\right)
+
\left(
    \begin{array}{c}
        1 \\
        \sin(t) \\
        \tan(t)
    \end{array}
\right)$`
