## Challenge
Evaluate the given integral along the indicated contour `$C$`.

`$$\int_C (2z + 3) dz$$` where `$C : z(t) = t + it^2, 0 \leq t \leq 2$`

## Solution Form
Your final answer will a complex number of the form `$x+iy$`. Enter `$x+y$` to check your answer.
