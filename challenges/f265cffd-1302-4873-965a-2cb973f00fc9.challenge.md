## Challenge
Find the solution for the system of equations
`$$
  \bm{x'} = \left(
    \begin{array}{cc}
       1 & 4 \\
       2 & 3
    \end{array}
  \right) \bm{x}
$$`

given the condition

`$$
\bm{x}(1) = \left(
    \begin{array}{c}
       0 \\
       3 
    \end{array}
  \right)
$$`


## Solution Form

To check your solution, sum the numbers in the vector `$x(0.9)$`.
