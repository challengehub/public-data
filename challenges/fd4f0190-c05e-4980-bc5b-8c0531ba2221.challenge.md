## Challenge
Find the solution for the system of equations
`$$
  \bm{x'} = \left(
    \begin{array}{cc}
      -1 & -1 \\
       1 & -1
    \end{array}
  \right) \bm{x}
$$`

given the initial condition

`$$
\bm{x}(0) = \left(
    \begin{array}{c}
      -4 \\
       2 
    \end{array}
  \right)
$$`


## Solution Form

To check your solution, sum the numbers in the vector `$x(0.9)$`.
